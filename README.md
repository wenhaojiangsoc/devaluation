# The Cultural Devaluation of Feminization Work

Repository for replication codes and data for the paper *The Cultural Devaluation of Feminized Work*, currently under review

## Overview about the Repository

This repository hosts intermediate data and the codes that I used to generate embedding vectors and main results of the paper. Because of the copyright restriction, I do not include the raw Google Ngram, the Corpus of Historical American English (COHA) or the Corpus of Contemporary American English (COCA).

The original Google Ngram data (American English), *ver.2*, however, are publicly available via:
[Google Ngram and Books, Version 2](http://storage.googleapis.com/books/ngrams/books/datasetsv2.html)

COHA and COCA can be purchased from:
[Corpus of Historical American English](https://www.english-corpora.org/coha/)
[Corpus of Contemporary American English](https://www.english-corpora.org/coca/)
